Product Description

This is an appropriate and mild shampoo for hydrating, nourishing, balancing and volumizing your hair. Enriched with natural oils and herbs, it earnestly cleans the scalp and hair and leaves the locks soft, smooth and lustrous.

This shampoo contains 27 high-quality botanicals elements to combine shampooing with advanced hair care. Being gentle on the scalp and hair, the cleansing agents in the clarifying shampoo can effectively remove stubborn dirt, grease, and product residues, thereby leaving your scalp and hair perfectly clean. Natural oils infused in the shampoo helps restore the scalps moisture barrier and hydrate it so that your hair does not feel dry after shampooing.
